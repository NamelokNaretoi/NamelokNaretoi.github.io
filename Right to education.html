FUTURE PROSPECTUS FOR THE PROGRAM:

Every Child has a Right to Education

Namelok-Naretoi wishes to continue with the Rehabilitation program in a much integrated way, as it would want to address many more areas of challenged persons / children in a diversified form, it would look other areas of children in the Maasai areas with different challenges.

With availability of funds, we also want to support orphan-children in our program, as well as Girls who are being abused sexually and those suffering from early marriages and Female Genital mutilation

( F G M). Most of our children in the program are orphans, many of their parents perished due to HIV/AIDS, and others were left by their grandmothers who passed off due to old age.
